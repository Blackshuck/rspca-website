Here at the RSPCA Cambridge we are dedicated to providing welfare services to animals and their owners.

We help injured strays and as many eligible pet owners as we possibly can. But we need your help.

Volunteers are urgently needed for shops, fostering, home visits and fundraising.

Donations help us continue our work and save animals.

Ask Our Virtual Assistant

If you have a question about animal welfare that's not covered by this site, try asking our virtual assistant. Click the assistant's icon to ask a question.

Our shops are in Mill Road and Burleigh Street, Cambridge and High Street, Newmarket.

Our clinic is available for pet owners on benefits.
