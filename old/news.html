RSPCA Cambridge news and information

For the latest news from the RSPCA Cambridge, follow us on Facebook and Twitter and take a look at our branch blog. There is also information on Google+ and Foursquare.

You can also make sure that you don’t miss out on the best bargains by following updates of the latest items available in our shops on Facebook, Twitter and the shops blog.

For information about the national RSPCA follow their regular Twitter updates with information about the latest events, laws and animals need rehoming. We also have an RSPCA Cambridge rehoming blog with pictures and details of animals needing a good new home.
