Donations

We rely very heavily on donations both of money and items that we can sell in any of the 3 RSPCA charity shops in the Cambridge area. Here’s what your money could go to:

  • £4 would board a cat for a day
  • £6 would board a dog for a day
  • £20 would pay for neutering a cat
  • £30 would buy a humane trap for catching feral cats so that they can be neutered
  • £95 would buy a microchip scanner

If you are a UK taxpayer, please Gift Aid your monetary donation. This means that for every £1 you donate, the RSPCA Cambridge receives an extra 25p. To do this simply choose your preferred method below. For more information on Gift Aid see the HRMC website.

Secure Online Donations

You can also donate to the RSPCA Cambridge & District online using our secure JustGiving form. Please remember to tick the Gift Aid box if you are a UK taxpayer. JustGiving is also a convenient way to set up a sponsorship page if you are considering doing a sponsored event to raise money for us.

Text Donations

You can even donate via a text message from your mobile phone. Simply text PETS00 £10 to 70070 to donate £10 to RSPCA Cambridge. Your phone network provider will add £10 to your bill and send £10 to our bank account. If you are a UK taxpayer and you complete the gift aid options we will receive an additional £2.50 at no cost to you.
